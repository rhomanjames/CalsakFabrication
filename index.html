Equipment
Explore our cutting-edge selection of precision machinery and tools tailored for every stage of plastics fabrication, ensuring unparalleled quality and efficiency in your production process.
  • CNC Routers

    Calsak Plastics has a high standard of production, meaning we produce high-quality routed parts on time. Creating a smooth routed edge on multiple substrates requires precision, experience, and proper equipment, all things Calsak Plastics takes pride in possessing as assets to our business.

  • Ovens/Forming Equipment

    This form of fabrication refers to transforming a plastic sheet into a three-dimensional shape using heat, vacuum, and pressure. Calsak Plastics has identified many advantages to thermoforming and has invested in its capabilities.

  • Die Cutters

    Die cutters are versatile machines used in various industries to precisely cut and shape materials such as paper, cardboard, fabric, and plastic. Calsak Plastics utilizes the sharp blades and customizable dies that allow die cutters to create intricate and consistent shapes, making them essential tools for manufacturing processes and crafting projects.

  • Lasers

    Lasers have revolutionized plastics fabrication, providing precise cutting, engraving, and marking techniques. They eliminate waste, save time, and offer versatility for intricate designs. Using controlled heat, lasers ensure clean edges and enhance efficiency in manufacturing.

  • CNC Panel Saws

    Calsak Plastics invests in automated cutting processes to maximize efficiency, reduce waste, and lower overall costs. Precision cutting creates tight tolerances, accuracy, and exceptional edge quality.

  • Heat Bending Tables

    Heat bending is a popular process among Calsak Plastics customers due to its low tooling costs, quick set-up process, and consistency of results. Heat bending can achieve multiple angles and configurations based on customer requests.

  • State of the Art (Clean) Acrylic Fabrication Room

    Our fabrication room is a pristine facility equipped with cutting-edge technology and meticulously controlled environment. It features advanced ventilation systems, temperature and humidity regulation, and specialized tools, enabling skilled craftsmen to create flawless acrylic products with exceptional clarity and precision.

  • Diamond Polisher & Flame Polishing

    We can produce flawless edge finishes with our polishing capabilities. Creating a clear optical edge on acrylic requires special skill that we are proud to possess in Charlotte. Acrylic is known for its clarity and light transmittance, but due to its fragile makeup, achieving optical clarity depends on the operator’s skill. We have mastered polishing techniques to ensure our customers’ expectations are exceeded.

  • Gluing/Solvent Welding

    Gluing, also known as solvent welding, is a common technique we use to join two or more plastic pieces together. By applying a solvent that dissolves the surface of the plastic, the molecules fuse and create a strong bond, resulting in a seamless and durable connection. Gluing or solvent welding offers a reliable and efficient method for assembling plastic components in various industries, from automotive and construction to electronics and packaging industries.
